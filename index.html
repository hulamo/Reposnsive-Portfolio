About

Hugo Moreno is the Founder and CEO of Opticas Ver De Verdad, the first low cost optical chain in Mexico with more than 101 stores in 11 differente cities in Mexico. Hugo is also an enthusiastic Web Developer.

Hugo has a Bacheleor in Civil Engineer form the Tecnologico de Monterrey and a MBA from IPADE Business School. Hugo has the Alumni Status from Harvard Business School with the PLD Program and has a Master Degree in Management with concentration in Business Coaching.
